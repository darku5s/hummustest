Valorant (2020)

Valorant is a free-to-play, first-person shooter game for online multiplayer. It was developed and published by Riot Games, the California-based company that also created League of Legends. The game is available on PC and Epic Games. In Valorant, two teams of five players compete in 25 rounds to plant or defuse a bomb. Each team is assigned to either attack or defend, and players control characters called "agents" who have different abilities based on cultures and countries around the world. Players use teamwork, gunplay, and tactical abilities to outwit and outplay their opponents. For example, some agents can equip gas emitters to throw toxic gas clouds, while others have bows and scouting abilities. Players can also carry sidearms and primary weapons, such as pistols, SMGs, shotguns, rifles, sniper rifles, and machine guns.
